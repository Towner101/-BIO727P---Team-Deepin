We are a team of Master's student at Queen Mary University of London, working on population genetics and bioinformatics. Our goal is to provide tools and resources for analyzing genetic data in the context of human population genetics. We believe that open and accessible tools are essential for scientific progress and we strive to make our work available to the wider scientific community.
